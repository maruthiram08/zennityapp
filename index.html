"Never Miss a Deal"

A modern, mobile-first credit card deals app designed for iOS, targeting credit card enthusiasts in India. Discover the best credit card offers, track your progress, and maximize your rewards with our intelligent stacking calculator.

SCREEN 1

Feed Screen

Browse hot deals, transfer bonuses, and welcome offers with time-sensitive badges and easy tracking options.

SCREEN 2

Card Detail

View active offers, card stats, and quick actions for each credit card in your portfolio.

SCREEN 3

My Tracker

Track your progress on active deals, monitor spending goals, and manage upcoming actions with visual progress bars.

SCREEN 4

Offers Browser

Explore trending offers across categories including stacking hacks, bank bonuses, and miles sales.

SCREEN 5

Stacking Calculator

Calculate maximum returns by stacking multiple offers, cards, and portals for optimal value.

SCREEN 6

Cards Portfolio

Manage your credit card collection with quick access to offers, fee due dates, and card details.

Design Features

  • Mobile-first iOS design with iPhone 14 Pro dimensions (393 x 852 pt)
  • Clean, card-based UI with generous white space and subtle shadows
  • SF Pro font family throughout for native iOS feel
  • Vibrant color system with Orange primary, Purple secondary, and status colors
  • Bottom tab navigation with 5 main sections
  • Time-sensitive deal badges (Hot, Transfer Bonus, Welcome Offer)
  • Progress tracking with visual progress bars
  • Intelligent stacking calculator for maximizing returns
  • High contrast for accessibility (minimum 4.5:1 ratio)
  • Comfortable tap targets (minimum 44x44pt)
  • Premium yet approachable aesthetic
  • Focus on scannability and quick actions
